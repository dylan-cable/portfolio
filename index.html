Who I am

About Me

I'm a B2B SaaS content marketer who's spent the last 4+ years building a brand from the ground up as the first marketing hire at Text Blaze. I don't just write content. I own the full funnel: keyword strategy, SEO, social, video production, influencer partnerships, paid ads, and community engagement.

What makes me different is that I combine a writer's instinct for messaging with an operator's eye for data. Every campaign I run is tied to a number, and every piece of content has a purpose. I thrive in startup environments where there's no playbook, just a problem to solve and a brand to build.

I graduated from UNC Chapel Hill's Hussman School of Journalism as a Wood Scholar and have been applying that foundation to metric-first marketing ever since.
